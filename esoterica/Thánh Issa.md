## Nicolas Notovitch Discovery (1894)

### The Story
- Russian journalist visiting Hemis monastery
- Broke leg, stayed to recover
- Monks showed ancient scrolls
- "Life of Saint Issa"

### The Manuscript Described
- Written in Pali
- 244 verses in 14 chapters
- Detailed Jesus's journey East
- Called him "Issa" (Arabic/Eastern name)
