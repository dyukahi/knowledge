## Brazil football facts

- **FIFA, “Neymar's return highlights Brazil squad”** — https://www.fifa.com/en/tournaments/mens/worldcup/canadamexicousa2026/articles/brazil-squad-announcement-carlo-ancelotti
  - Published 19 May 2026; announcement event at Museum of Tomorrow, Rio de Janeiro. Other outlets timestamped the squad news on 18 May.
  - Ancelotti named Brazil's 26-man squad with Neymar included after no Brazil call-up since 2023 and after hamstring/knee injury concerns.
  - FIFA wording: Neymar will play his fourth World Cup; squad also includes Vinicius Junior, Raphinha, Marquinhos, Gabriel Martinelli and Gabriel Magalhaes.
  - Ancelotti quote before announcement: “We will have a list from which we can develop quality football, with a collective spirit”; he emphasized resilience over a “perfect team”.
  - FIFA notes Brazil's five titles and a 24-year drought since 2002; the 1970→1994 drought was also 24 years and ended in USA.
